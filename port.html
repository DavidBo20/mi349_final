The Ports

PC ports are physical connectors on a computer that allow it to communicate with external devices, such as monitors, keyboards, mice, and storage drives. These ports come in various types, each designed for specific purposes, like USB for data transfer and peripheral connection, HDMI for video and audio output, Ethernet for network connections, and Audio jacks for sound input and output. Ports are essential for expanding the functionality of a computer and connecting it to a variety of devices.

What do Ports do?

PC ports allow a computer to connect and communicate with external devices. They enable the transfer of data, video, audio, and power to peripherals like monitors, keyboards, mice, printers, and storage devices. Each port type serves a specific function, such as USB for data transfer, HDMI for video/audio output, and Ethernet for network connections. These ports expand a computer's capabilities and allow it to interact with a wide range of devices.

What is the price range?

Since ports are included with the PC case, the price range for ports would be the same as the case price rage. Although, almost all pc cases should come with ports, meaning these should be avaliable at all price ranges.
