Welcome to the Computer Parts Information Site!

The Computer Parts Information Site, or C.P.I.S for short, is a website with the function of describing the different components of a personal computer. This website has intractable images and buttons to bring you to different sections of the website. There are explanations of every part of the PC you may encounter during the building process.
